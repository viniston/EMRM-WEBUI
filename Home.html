Sync Events To Your Calendar

You can sync your events with Outlook, Google Calendar, Apple Calendar or any calendar that allows you to subscribe by URL. A few things to note:

  • You will need to use Resource Guru to add or edit events. Your calendar will just display them as "read only".
  • Only events where you have been booked will be synced.
  • Your calendar software will only refresh every now and then. We recommend you set a refresh rate of every 5 mins if possible. If you can't configure this in your calendar then it may take up to 24 hours to refresh itself.
  • Only share your calendar URL with people you trust. If you think it's fallen into the wrong hands,
    to regenerate your URL.
  • You can find detailed instructions on integrating specific calendars in our Help Center.
